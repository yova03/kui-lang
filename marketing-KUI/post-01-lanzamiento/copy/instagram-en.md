## Post caption (≤2200 chars)

KUI was born from a simple discomfort: academic research shouldn't fight its compiler.

It's a document language designed for theses, papers and reports. One line of text becomes a numbered figure, a captioned table, a native chart. The PDF compiles directly — no LaTeX, no intermediate HTML.

The first thesis shipped in KUI is an archaeology dissertation at UNSAAC (Cusco, Peru). Modular chapters, bibliography, TOC and automatic figures — all from a single `main.kui`.

Open source. Validator, templates and renderer are available for review and contribution.
